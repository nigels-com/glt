NBody
-----

1. Mouse
--------

Use the mouse to zoom, pan and rotate.

2. Keyboard commands
--------------------

Demos

   SHIFT+1  1-Body Gravity
   SHIFT+2  3-Body Gravity
   SHIFT+3  N-Body Gravity
   SHIFT+4  Solarsystem
   SHIFT+5  Lorenz
   SHIFT+6  Lorenz 2-Body
   SHIFT+7  Lorenz Random Points
   SHIFT+8  Lorenz Line of points
   SHIFT+9  Rossler
   SHIFT+0  Rossler line of points

Animation

   s        Start or stop
   .        Speed-up
   ,        Slow-down
   SPACE    Step

Integration

   I        Select Integrator

        Euler
        Midpoint
        Euler-Cromell

Display

   i        Onscreen information
   p        Display points/spheres
   a        Acceleration visualisation
   c        Center of mass
   l        Lighting
   x        x,y,z Axes

   t        Body trail visualisation
   </>      Adjust trail length

   v        Force field visualisation
   +/-      Adjust force field detail

Follow

   f        Follow mode
   0..9     Follow body n
